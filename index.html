About the Club

Developing
Girls' Football

Achilles Girls FC is a dedicated girls' football club committed to developing talented young players in a fun, supportive, and competitive environment. All coaches are fully FA qualified DBS checked.

Playing in our iconic red and black vertical stripes, our teams compete in the Suffolk Girls & Women's Football League (SGWFL), giving every player the chance to develop their skills and love of the game.

We currently field three teams — our U14 Pumas, U13 Flamingos, and our brand new U12 Albion — as the club continues to grow and thrive.
